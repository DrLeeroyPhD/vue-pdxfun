About PDX Fun

We are a locally run, family operated business focused on having fun!

Hello! I’m Kathryn!
I ran a nationally roaming event production company for over 10 years for clients such as Hello Kitty and Subaru, we built massive campaigns and art happenings from scratch; it was exhausting! So I had this great idea – to stick closer to home, and launch an earthquake preparedness company. I came up with a structure and plan, and got ready to roll it out. …and then the 2016 election hit, and everyone was inundated with negativity seemingly all of the time, and then we were all exhausted. So I had this other great idea! To make whimsical never-before-seen party rental items with my amazing network of artists and creative vendors from around the world. That seemed like a way better plan. We look forward to working with you, and to continuously improving upon our inventory.
Play Nice and Have a blast!

-Kathryn, and Max and Benjamin and Ham and Fu and Dave and Raisin
